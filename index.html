Global Brand Ambassador Campaign

A Comprehensive Analysis Repository

Department of Culture and Tourism - Abu Dhabi

This repository provides comprehensive documentation and analysis of Abu Dhabi's Global Brand Ambassador campaign featuring Chris Hemsworth and Elsa Pataky (Nov 2024 to Mar 2025). The campaign represents a strategic shift in destination marketing, combining celebrity endorsement with data-driven precision to address perception barriers and drive measurable brand outcomes.

The analysis spans strategic foundations, campaign execution, brand impact, paid media performance, market comparisons, and performance assessment. This deep-dive focuses on four Tier 1 markets (United States, United Kingdom, Germany, and France), with campaign data available across 27 markets globally. These reports examine Abu Dhabi's approach to attribution in celebrity-led destination marketing.
