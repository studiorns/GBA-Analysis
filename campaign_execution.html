1.3 Launch Tactics Breakdown

Tactic 1: Global Newswire + Direct Market Outreach

Execution: Simultaneous press release distribution to 60 markets combined with direct pitching to key journalists

Result: Comprehensive coverage across entertainment, lifestyle, and travel verticals

Learning: Combination approach (newswire + direct outreach) ensures no market gaps

Tactic 2: Embargoed Exclusive Interview

Execution: Conde Nast Traveller exclusive interview with Elsa Pataky and Chris Hemsworth, embargoed until official announcement

Result: Premium placement in Tier 1 publication, launched simultaneously with partnership announcement

Learning: Exclusive placements create media value beyond standard press release coverage

Tactic 3: In-Person Industry Reveal (WTM)

Execution: DCT reveals partnership on WTM stage, film screened to industry audience

Result: Extended reach beyond media to industry participants, garnered attention from other destinations

Learning: Industry events amplify campaign reach to B2B audiences and create secondary buzz

Part 5: What We Learned

5.1 What Worked

Celebrity-Led Launch Strategy

Finding: 106M impressions in first 72 hours (700% higher than Summer 2024), Chris/Elsa content outperformed ALL other global celebrity posts within 24 hours.

Recommendation: Continue using celebrity-led launch approach in future campaigns for exceptional visibility burst.

Meta & Google Search Excellence

Finding: Consistently demonstrated as key platforms for driving sessions and reach with high volume at low cost.

Recommendation: Continue prioritizing Meta and Google Search while scaling TikTok investment.

Premium Platform VTR Maintenance

Finding: Epsilon, Amazon, CTV sustained high View Through Rates throughout campaign.

Recommendation: Allocate more budget to premium video platforms for mid/lower-funnel messaging.

Sentiment Management Success

Finding: Positive sentiment improved from 59.3% to 69.0% (+17%), negative sentiment reduced from 7.5% to 3.0% (-60%).

Recommendation: Maintain active sentiment monitoring and content adjustment protocols.

Cultural Storytelling + Celebrity Appeal

Finding: Blend of traditional Emirati culture with celebrity family adventures drove strong engagement.

Recommendation: Continue cultural storytelling via Instagram Stories with interactive elements.

5.2 Operational Challenges

Creative Fatigue (Critical Learning)

Challenge: Strong launch performance followed by creative fatigue from January onward, leading to declining VTRs and rising CPVs.

Timeline: November (peak) → December (phase shift) → January onward (fatigue)

Recommendation: For long-term campaigns, plan monthly creative refresh cycles at design stage.

Timing & Access Constraints

Challenge 1: Contractual and scheduling constraints limited strategic comms team access to talent.

Challenge 2: Campaign platform finalized very close to launch, insufficient lead time.

Challenge 3: Launch coincided with US elections (limited media interest).

Recommendation: Allocate 8-12 weeks lead time for platform development and talent coordination.

Influencer Execution Challenges

Challenge 1: Influencers not keen on simply amplifying pre-produced assets (felt inauthentic).

Challenge 2: Insufficient Ambassador branding at locations during influencer visits.

Challenge 3: GBA itinerary connection not always explicit in final content.

Recommendation: Allow influencers creative freedom, strengthen on-site branding, develop clearer briefing materials.
