ROI Methodology & Insights

Composite ROI Score Formula: Weighted combination of (1) Brand Health Growth (30%), (2) Barrier Improvement Rate (25%), (3) Ad Diagnostics Win Rate (25%), and (4) Media Effectiveness (20%). Scores normalized 0-100 across markets for comparison.

UK (29.1 - Best Balanced): Leads execution quality with 67% barrier improvement and 100% ad diagnostics wins. Despite moderate media effectiveness (13.23), superior creative performance delivers best overall ROI balance.

USA (27.7 - Growth + Efficiency): Dominates media effectiveness (18.39 points/1B impressions) despite 2.19x higher CPM than France. Highest brand growth (4.8pp) validates that creative quality trumps media costs.

Germany (23.4 - Media Efficient): Strong media effectiveness (16.96, ranked 2nd) but weakest consideration conversion (+0.5pp) limits overall score. Requires mid-funnel creative optimization.

France (19.8 - Needs Optimization): Paradox of cost efficiency: Best cost per total brand health point ($423K) but weakest media effectiveness (12.54) and ad diagnostics (33%). Low CPM cannot compensate for creative execution gaps.

Part 2: Strategic Insights & Future Campaign Recommendations

Market-Specific Learnings

UK: Sustain Dominance Strategy

Performance Summary:

  • Best overall performer: 67% barrier improvement rate (4 of 6 barriers)
  • Ad diagnostics dominance: 100% win rate (3 of 3 metrics)
  • Highest consideration lift: +7.5pp with +0.9pp celebrity advantage
  • Brand association leader: 37% (highest across all markets)

Future Campaign Recommendations:

  • No major strategy changes needed - current approach validates celebrity effectiveness
  • Continue investment at current levels; UK demonstrates highest ROI across all metrics
  • Replicate UK creative framework in other markets for consistency
  • Maintain celebrity visibility with 8-12 week creative refresh cycles

USA: Capitalize on Growth Momentum

Performance Summary:

  • Highest total growth: 4.8 points (awareness +1.9pp, consideration +2.0pp)
  • Strongest ad appeal: 93% (highest across all markets and campaigns)
  • Family positioning strength: -4.0pp children entertainment improvement
  • Quality traffic leader: +124% time spent on site from META

Future Campaign Recommendations:

  • Amplify family-focused content: Children entertainment barrier shows proven success
  • Maintain balanced awareness + consideration messaging that drove growth
  • Leverage 93% ad appeal advantage through sustained celebrity visibility
  • Priority: Address safety perception worsening (+5.8pp) through PR coordination
  • Consider Chris Hemsworth family content given strong children entertainment results

Germany: Fix Awareness-to-Consideration Gap

Performance Summary:

  • Awareness success: +1.8pp growth (tied for highest)
  • Consideration failure: +0.5pp (lowest across all markets)
  • Conversion disconnect: 3.6x gap between awareness and consideration growth
  • Creative wear-out: -40% META VTR decline indicates rapid fatigue

Future Campaign Recommendations:

  • Problem diagnosis: Awareness is working; consideration conversion is not
  • Test message variants emphasizing rational benefits and specific experiences
  • Address authenticity concern (+2.1pp worsening) with destination credibility content
  • Aggressive creative refresh: 4-8 week cycles to combat rapid fatigue
  • Do not increase awareness investment; focus on mid-funnel optimization
  • Consider localized German celebrity partnerships to complement Chris Hemsworth

France: Enhance Creative Appeal & Association

Performance Summary:

  • Strong intent conversion: +1.6pp (highest intent lift)
  • Bottom-funnel strength: +0.9pp consideration lift advantage
  • Ad diagnostics weakness: 60% appeal, 19% association (both lowest)
  • Memory quality gap: 10% "definitely remember" (lowest by far)

Future Campaign Recommendations:

  • Address top-funnel creative execution gaps: Lowest ad appeal (60%) and brand association (19%) indicate creative isn't resonating despite strong bottom-funnel performance
  • Test localized creative variants with French cultural references and complementary French influencer partnerships
  • Preserve bottom-funnel momentum - strong intent growth validates current conversion messaging
  • Address children entertainment concern (+4.3pp worsening) with family-focused content
  • META platform strength: -61% CPV efficiency (best across all markets) - increase META budget allocation

Cross-Market Strategic Lessons

What Worked: Validated Successes

  • English-speaking market advantage: UK (67% barrier improvement) and USA (4.8 total growth points) demonstrate strongest performance, suggesting cultural and linguistic affinity with Chris Hemsworth drives superior resonance
  • Celebrity appeal superiority: 67% ad diagnostics win rate (8 of 12 metrics) validates celebrity strategy
  • META cost efficiency: All markets achieved CPV improvements (-18% to -61%), making META ideal for reach-focused objectives
  • Quality traffic generation: 3 of 4 markets showed substantial time-on-site improvements (+63% to +124%) from META
  • Brand decline reversal: Only campaign period to recover consideration to pre-crisis levels (+2.5pp from Q3'24 low)

What Didn't Work: Critical Challenges

  • Safety perception deterioration: Worsened in 3 of 4 markets (USA +5.8pp, Germany +5.1pp, France +2.7pp) due to external geopolitical factors beyond marketing control
  • YouTube cost escalation: CPV increased +93% to +232% across all markets, undermining platform ROI despite strong engagement
  • Creative wear-out variability: Germany (-40% META VTR) declined 2.2x faster than USA (-18%), indicating market-specific fatigue rates
  • France creative execution gap: Lowest ad appeal (60%) and association (19%) despite strong intent conversion suggests localization needs

Platform-Specific Insights

META Platform: Cost Efficiency Champion

Validated Strengths:

  • Universal cost efficiency: All markets achieved CPV reductions (-18% to -61%)
  • Quality traffic: 3 of 4 markets showed time-on-site gains (+63% to +124%)
  • Benchmark compliance: All markets maintained 25-40% VTR range

Future Campaign Guidance:

  • Budget allocation: Prioritize France (-61% CPV) and USA (-42% CPV) for cost-efficient reach
  • Creative refresh cycles: Germany requires 4-8 weeks; USA tolerates 8-12 weeks
  • Recommended split: 60-70% of total paid budget to META for awareness objectives

YouTube Platform: Engagement vs. Cost Trade-off

Validated Strengths:

  • CTR improvement trajectory: Substantial growth (+91% to +360%) across all markets
  • Above-benchmark VTR: 40.53%-48.60% vs 32% industry standard (+27% to +52%)
  • Engagement consistency: Sustained performance throughout campaign period

Critical Challenges:

  • Cost escalation: CPV increased +93% to +232% across all markets
  • ROI pressure: Higher costs require justification via engagement quality

Future Campaign Guidance:

  • Strategic use: Deploy for high-intent engagement (CTR) vs broad reach (CPV)
  • Format optimization: 30s assets generated +631% CTR in France, +501% in USA
  • Recommended split: 30-40% of total paid budget to YouTube for engagement objectives
  • Balance YouTube's +360% CTR gains against META's -61% CPV efficiency

Final Recommendations Summary

Bottom Line for Future Celebrity Campaigns

Continue celebrity strategy with market-specific optimizations: UK requires no changes (67% barrier improvement validates approach), USA should amplify family content (+124% quality traffic), Germany needs aggressive creative refresh (4-8 week cycles) and mid-funnel focus, France requires localized creative to improve 60% ad appeal and 19% association rates while preserving strong +1.6pp intent growth. Platform allocation: 60-70% META for cost-efficient reach, 30-40% YouTube for high-intent engagement. Safety concerns require cross-functional PR response beyond marketing scope.
